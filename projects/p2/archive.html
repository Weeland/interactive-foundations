About

North America COC is a TRPG group in Tencent QQ that provides resources and hosts game events for COC (Call of Cthulhu) players and keepers from China. Players can choose to join the game they are interested in through announcements, and conversely, keepers can contact the admins to announce their game. This site records the events and games available for participation and watching.
