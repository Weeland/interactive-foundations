5th Module Contest

Deadline 12/25/2021
Location: Tencent QQ

The theme of this module competition is "recession." This topic does not need to be the module's theme; related context or vocabulary also counts. Those who deduct the topic will get extra points in the selection. To enter, send the module to the administration and indicate that you are entering. After the selection, feedback on the module will be given, and the winners upload their modules to the "Great Works" folder and receive a jar of special April sauce.

About

North America COC is a TRPG group in Tencent QQ that provides resources and hosts game events for COC (Call of Cthulhu) players and keepers from China. Players can choose to join the game they are interested in through announcements, and conversely, keepers can contact the admins to announce their game. This site records the events and games available for participation and watching.
